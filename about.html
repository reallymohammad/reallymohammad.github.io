About Us

Welcome to Custom Carpentry & Renovation Company, your premier choice for custom-built solutions in the Leesburg, Virginia area and throughout the D.C., Maryland, and Virginia (DMV) region. We specialize in transforming your living spaces with bespoke craftsmanship, tailored to meet your unique needs.

From closets (master closets, walk-in closets, kids' closets and etc), to pantries, mudrooms, and storage solutions (garage storage, under stairs storage and etc), we bring your vision to life. Whether you're looking to renovate your office, design a custom entertainment center, or create a functional and stylish storage solution, our team has the expertise and dedication to deliver exceptional results.

We take pride in designing spaces that create a "Fair Place" in your home, ensuring every element is perfectly placed to maximize both functionality and aesthetic appeal. Whether it's a well-organized pantry, a cozy mudroom, or an efficient office renovation, we ensure that every project is executed with precision and care.

At Custom Carpentry & Renovation Company, we understand that your home is your sanctuary, and we strive to make it as beautiful and functional as possible. Let us help you create the space of your dreams with our custom-built solutions that are as unique as you are.
